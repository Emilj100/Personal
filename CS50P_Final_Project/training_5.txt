Here is your training program:

5 Days per Week - Push, Pull, Legs, Upper, Lower

- Day 1 (Push):
    - Cardio: 20 min choice of running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
    - Bench Press: 4 x 8-10
    - Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 x 10
    - Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 x 12
    - Dumbbell Lateral Raises: 3 x 12
    - Tricep Pushdowns: 3 x 10

- Day 2 (Pull):

    - Cardio: 20 min choice of running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
    - Pull-Ups: 3 x 10
    - Seated Rows: 3 x 10
    - Lat Pulldown: 3 x 10
    - Face Pulls: 3 x 12
    - Bicep Curls: 3 x 12

- Day 3 (Legs):

    - Cardio: 20 min choice of running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
    - Squats: 4 x 10
    - Deadlifts: 3 x 8
    - Lunges: 3 x 12
    - Leg Press: 3 x 12
    - Calf Raises: 3 x 15

- Day 4 (Upper Body):

    - Cardio: 20 min choice of running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
    - Bench Press: 3 x 8-10
    - Dumbbell Rows: 3 x 10
    - Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 x 10
    - Bicep Curls: 3 x 12
    - Tricep Extensions: 3 x 10

- Day 5 (Lower Body):

    - Cardio: 20 min choice of running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
    - Deadlifts: 3 x 8
    - Bulgarian Split Squats: 3 x 10
    - Leg Curls: 3 x 12
    - Calf Raises: 4 x 15

Here is your training program:

3 Days per Week - Upper, Lower, and Full Body Program

- Day 1 (Upper Body):

    - Cardio: 20 min choice of running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
    - Bench Press: 3 x 8-10
    - Dumbbell Rows: 3 x 10
    - Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 x 12
    - Lat Pulldown: 3 x 10
    - Bicep Curls: 3 x 10
    - Tricep Pushdowns: 3 x 12

- Day 2 (Lower Body):

    - Cardio: 20 min choice of running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
    - Squats: 4 x 10
    - Deadlifts: 3 x 8
    - Lunges: 3 x 12
    - Leg Press: 3 x 12
    - Calf Raises: 3 x 15
    - Leg Curl: 3 x 10

- Day 3 (Full Body):

    - Cardio: 20 min choice of running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
    - Deadlifts: 3 x 8
    - Pull-ups: 3 x 10
    - Dumbbell Bench Press: 3 x 12
    - Seated Rows: 3 x 10
    - Russian Twists: 3 x 20
    - Bicycle Crunches: 3 x 20

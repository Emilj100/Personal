2 Days per Week - Full Body Programs (Two Variations)

    - Cardio: 20 min choice of running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine

- Day 2:

    - Cardio: 20 min choice of running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
    - Deadlifts: 3 x 8
    - Overhead Shoulder Press: 3 x 10
    - Lat Pulldown: 3 x 10
    - Leg Press: 3 x 12
    - Tricep Extensions: 3 x 10
    - Russian Twists: 3 x 20

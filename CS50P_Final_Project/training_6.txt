Here is your training program:

6 Days per Week - Push, Pull, Legs (2 Variations)

Day 1 (Push - Variation 1):

- Cardio: 20 min of choice running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
- Bench Press: 4 x 8-10
- Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 x 12
- Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 x 10
- Dumbbell Lateral Raises: 3 x 15
- Tricep Pushdowns: 3 x 12

Day 2 (Pull - Variation 1):

- Cardio: 20 min of choice running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
- Pull-Ups: 4 x 8-10
- Seated Cable Rows: 3 x 12
- Lat Pulldown: 3 x 10
- Face Pulls: 3 x 12
- Bicep Curls: 3 x 12

Day 3 (Legs - Variation 1):

- Cardio: 20 min of choice running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
- Squats: 4 x 10
- Leg Press: 3 x 12
- Walking Lunges: 3 x 12 (each leg)
- Calf Raises: 4 x 15
- Leg Curls: 3 x 12

Day 4 (Push - Variation 2):

- Cardio: 20 min of choice running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
- Overhead Press: 4 x 8
- Decline Bench Press: 3 x 10
- Cable Chest Flyes: 3 x 12
- Dumbbell Front Raises: 3 x 12
- Overhead Tricep Extension: 3 x 10

Day 5 (Pull - Variation 2):

- Cardio: 20 min of choice running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
- Bent Over Rows: 4 x 10
- T-Bar Rows: 3 x 10
- Straight-Arm Pulldown: 3 x 12
- Rear Delt Flyes: 3 x 15
- Hammer Curls: 3 x 10

Day 6 (Legs - Variation 2):

- Cardio: 20 min of choice running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
- Deadlifts: 4 x 8
- Bulgarian Split Squats: 3 x 10 (each leg)
- Hack Squat: 3 x 10
- Seated Calf Raises: 4 x 15
- Leg Extensions: 3 x 12

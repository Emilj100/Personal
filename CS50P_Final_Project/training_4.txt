Here is your training program:

4 Days per Week - 2 Upper, 2 Lower

- Day 1 (Upper Body):

    - Cardio: 20 min choice of running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
    - Bench Press: 4 x 8-10
    - Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 x 12
    - Lat Pulldown: 3 x 10
    - Bicep Curls: 3 x 12
    - Tricep Pushdowns: 3 x 12

- Day 2 (Lower Body):

    - Cardio: 20 min choice of running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
    - Squats: 4 x 10
    - Leg Press: 3 x 12
    - Lunges: 3 x 12
    - Calf Raises: 3 x 15
    - Leg Extensions: 3 x 10

- Day 3 (Upper Body):

    - Cardio: 20 min choice of running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
    - Incline Dumbbell Press: 4 x 10
    - Seated Rows: 3 x 10
    - Dumbbell Lateral Raises: 3 x 12
    - Hammer Curls: 3 x 12
    - Skull Crushers: 3 x 10

- Day 4 (Lower Body):

    - Cardio: 20 min choice of running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
    - Deadlifts: 3 x 8
    - Bulgarian Split Squats: 3 x 10
    - Leg Curls: 3 x 12
    - Calf Raises: 4 x 15

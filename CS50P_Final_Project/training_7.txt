Here is your training program:

7 Days per Week - Full Body Rotation

For en 7-dages program, som giver variation og balancerer muskelgrupperne over ugen, kan hver dag være en blanding af øvre og nedre kropsøvelser med moderat intensitet for at undgå overbelastning.

Day 1:

- Cardio: 20 min choice running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
- Squats: 4 x 10
- Dumbbell Bench Press: 3 x 12
- Lat Pulldown: 3 x 10
- Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 x 10
- Russian Twists: 3 x 20

Day 2:

- Cardio: 20 min choice running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
- Deadlifts: 3 x 8
- Seated Rows: 3 x 10
- Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 x 10
- Lunges: 3 x 12 (each leg)
- Bicycle Crunches: 3 x 20

Day 3:

- Cardio: 20 min choice running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
- Leg Press: 3 x 12
- Overhead Shoulder Press: 3 x 10
- Pull-Ups: 3 x 8-10
- Dumbbell Flyes: 3 x 12
- Plank: 3 x 1 min

Day 4:

- Cardio: 20 min choice running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
- Squats: 4 x 10
- Lat Pulldown: 3 x 10
- Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 x 10
- Calf Raises: 3 x 15
- Russian Twists: 3 x 20

Day 5:

- Cardio: 20 min choice running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
- Deadlifts: 3 x 8
- Dumbbell Rows: 3 x 10
- Bench Press: 3 x 10
- Lunges: 3 x 12 (each leg)
- Bicycle Crunches: 3 x 20

Day 6:

- Cardio: 20 min choice running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
- Leg Press: 3 x 12
- Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 x 10
- Cable Rows: 3 x 10
- Calf Raises: 3 x 15
- Plank: 3 x 1 min

Day 7:

- Cardio: 20 min choice running, cycling, stairmaster, or rowing machine
- Goblet Squats: 4 x 12
- Incline Dumbbell Bench Press: 3 x 10
- Seated Rows: 3 x 10
- Dumbbell Lateral Raises: 3 x 12
- Russian Twists: 3 x 20
